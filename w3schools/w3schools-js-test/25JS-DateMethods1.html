JavaScript Date Methods

Date methods let you get and set date values (years, months, days, hours, minutes, seconds, milliseconds)

Date Get Methods

Get methods are used for getting a part of a date. Here are the most common (alphabetically): getDate(), getDay(), getFullYear(), getHours(), getMilliseconds(), getMinutes(), getMonth(), getSeconds() and getTime().
